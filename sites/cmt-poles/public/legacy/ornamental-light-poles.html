Legacy ornamental light poles are crafted in many classic-styles with rust-proof, corrosion-resistant composite material, which provides enduring beauty. These decorative lighting structures offer low-cost advantages compared to other materials and are virtually maintenance free which saves time and money over the product lifetime.

A key differentiator of CMT Legacy lighting pole products is that many of our composite poles and shrouds are crafted as one-piece structures, resulting in an integrated pole and shroud combination. In addition to providing long-lasting good looks and exemplary structural integrity, the pole is easy to install using either a direct-burial approach or an anchor-base foundation. The singular Legacy design eliminates the risk of base cover theft. Also, public safety is enhanced since the entire unit is a continuous, non-conductive, fiberglass composite structure. Contact the CMT factory to learn more.

Legacy decorative light poles are easy to install. The lightweight poles can be carried and installed without lifting equipment. The composite structure can be directly buried into most any soil with proper backfill and tamping techniques. The poles are well suited for any outdoor environment and the poles are particularly valuable for sites where the grounds must not be disturbed and for out-of-the-way and hard-to-reach locations, such as parks and trails.

The composite construction of Legacy poles also offers safety benefits due to the non-conductive nature of the material. The poles are ideal for highly populated venues such as ball parks, playgrounds, residential areas and commercial shopping and business properties.

CMT Legacy poles add a level of sophistication to any lighting project. Round Tapered and Round Straight configurations are available and many poles are also available with fluted designs for a distinctive appearance. Octagonal-shaped poles are available in mounting heights up to 20 ft. Contact CMT to learn more. Classic acorn or lantern fixtures, available from many manufacturers, provide an extra touch of elegance.

CMT Legacy customized decorative lamp posts and light poles

The Legacy light pole product line allows for innovative customization opportunities. Custom-designed outdoor decorative lighting is installed to create a distinctive sense of community in suburban areas and small towns, to facilitate urban redevelopment in metropolitan areas, and to provide an upscale aesthetic on important or historic roads and bridges.
