CMT Decorative Product Selector & Decorative Specifications

The Decorative Product Selector allows you to choose the CMT decorative poles and base covers that best meet your lighting project needs.

Easy access to CMT decorative product specification pages:

POLES: Select a decorative light pole model. The base covers available with that pole will display. Click on the desired model number to access the specification page you need.

BASE COVERS: You may select a decorative base cover first, if you prefer. The poles available with that base cover will display. Click on the desired model number to access the specification page you need.

RESET: To look up a new pole and base cover combination, click the RESET button.
