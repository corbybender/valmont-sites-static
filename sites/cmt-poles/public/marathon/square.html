Square light poles

Marathon composite square lighting standards



CMT straight square light poles are available for direct-burial or anchor-base installations. Square lighting poles from CMT provide an affordable, stylish and remarkably durable solution for today's modern commercial area lighting, street lighting and other outdoor lighting applications.

Engineered with a high strength-to-weight ratio, CMT square poles feature a lightweight composite construction that is easy to install and built to last. Customers can choose from these standard size pole configurations:

  • 4 in. x 4 in. square poles in mounting heights up to 25 ft.
  • 5 in. x 5 in. square poles in mounting heights up to 30 ft.

Our most popular colors are black, bronze, white and grey. Custom colors are available. Consult factory for details.

For a limited time, CMT Marathon square light poles are available with reduced lead times and special pricing. Download the CMT square light pole promotional sheet to learn more and contact CMT for pricing.

CMT Square Light Poles - Product Benefits

  • Low lifetime maintenance - regardless of humidity, CMT lighting poles will not rust, rot, or corrode.
  • Sturdy, yet lightweight, CMT square light poles can be hand-carried and installed without lifting equipment.
  • Long life durability - dent-resistant structures.
  • With UV inhibitors integrated into the composite resin, CMT square poles provide enduring beauty.
  • Standard colors are black, bronze and grey.
  • Smooth, attractive surface appeals to architects and commercial property owners.
  • CMT poles feature a non-conductive composite design for safety.
