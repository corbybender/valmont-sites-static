In addition to outdoor lighting applications, Marathon poles can be designed for any number of outdoor applications in either direct-burial or pedestal-mount installations. CMT specialty products are engineered to meet your exacting needs.

With decades of experience in engineering and producing centrifugally cast composite poles, CMT is a natural choice for providing custom and specialty products to satisfy our customer's specific needs.

Whenever and wherever a tough, durable and cost-effective pole is needed, Marathon composite structures should be considered for the installation. Our Marathon pole is lightweight and strong with low EMI characteristic and a non-conductive, non-corrosive finish. Marathon poles can be designed for both direct burial or pedestal mount installations. Contact CMT to learn more about our custom capabilities.

Examples of custom-built products and specialty applications include: 
  • Custom lighting poles
  • Utility power distribution poles
  • Solar-panel structures combined with lighting and/or school-zone signs
  • Wind-energy poles
  • Security-camera structures
  • Telecommunication poles
  • Wireless communication structures
  • Electric vehicle charging station support structures
  • Bollard structures for safety perimeters
  • Way-finding sign structures in urban and historic settings
  • Hike and bike trail sign structures and guideposts
