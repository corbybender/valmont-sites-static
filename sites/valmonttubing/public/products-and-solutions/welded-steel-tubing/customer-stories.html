The Voice of our Customers

 

Across agriculture, industrial, pneumatic, grain handling and transportation markets, our welded steel tubing plays a critical role in essential equipment. From grain handling systems to air compressors and conveyors, customers trust Valmont Tubing for durability, precision, and performance. But don’t just take our word for it, hear from the businesses that rely on our tubing every day.
“Valmont Tubing has consistently been a reliable supplier for our grain handling accessories. As a manufacturer and distributor, we require high-quality round welded seam tubing for our customers, primarily millwrights and contractors who install the tubing. The ability to get large quantities of tubing quickly and efficiently has been a key factor in our 20-year partnership with Valmont Tubing.
The quality of the steel tubing is always excellent, and their delivery timelines are always reliable. In addition, their communication with both the inside and outside sales teams has made our experience smooth and seamless.” - President of a leading manufacturer and supplier of grain spouting for the agricultural industry.
“We are a custom manufacturer of vacuum arches for the car wash industry, and keeping costs down on raw carbon steel tubing is crucial for us. We began working with Valmont Tubing three years ago after being referred by one of their customers, and we’ve been very impressed. Their ability to provide custom cut lengths has been a game-changer for our operations. Valmont consistently delivers high-quality tubing that meets our specific needs, and their customer service has been excellent. They are always willing to go the extra mile to accommodate our requirements.” - Production Planner/Buyer for a leading manufacturer of carwash central vacuum systems.  
“We’ve been working with Valmont Tubing for decades and started when we were searching for a specific tube size that wasn’t commonly stocked by service centers. Over the years, we've primarily purchased structural steel tubing for manufacturing equipment serving the Ag, RV, trailer, and other markets. Their high-quality products have been integral to the success of our projects, consistently meeting our custom specifications. Their customer service has great communication and collaboration. In a recent project, their team even initiated video calls with engineering, quality, and sales to ensure the product would meet our exact needs.” - Dave Damstra, Buyer at Demco, a company that manufactures a variety of products, including agricultural equipment, semi-trailers, RV towing products, and trailer components.  
“We’ve been working with Valmont Tubing for over 12 years. As a supplier of underground utility, water well, and septic products in Colorado Springs, having a reliable vendor for steel tubing is crucial.
