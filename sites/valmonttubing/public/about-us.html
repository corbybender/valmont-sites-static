About Valmont Tubing
In 1953, Valley Manufacturing acquired the patent for the center pivot irrigation system. It was during this time that Daugherty realized the need for a lighter, higher quality pipe, leading to the establishment of Valmont Tubing and its steel pipe and tubing operations. Originally focused on providing solutions for irrigation, Valmont Tubing has since expanded its reach to serve a wide range of industries. Today, in addition to the agricultural sector, we support diverse markets such as transportation, pneumatic, exhaust, construction, structural, and many other industrial applications.
Markets We Serve
Valmont Tubing is a leading producer of welded steel tubing, serving diverse markets including agriculture, industrial, and pneumatic applications. Our tubing is utilized in equipment such as grain handling systems, air compressors, conveyors, and water heaters. With options ranging from large OD light wall tubing to ultra-low carbon and HSLA grades, we provide a variety of finishes—including hot rolled, galvanized, stainless, and more—to meet stringent requirements and evolving customer needs. About Valmont Industries
Valmont is here to modernize vital infrastructure and increase agricultural productivity, sustainably and reliably. We galvanize steel to last a century with minimal environmental impact. We modernize irrigation to feed a growing population by utilizing the latest technology. We harden the electrical grid to enable the delivery of reliable power to millions of homes around the world. And we help make communities everywhere safer, cleaner and more connected through our smart infrastructure technology.
Valmont serves two primary segments — agriculture and infrastructure — with seven markets we serve: Utility, Lighting and Transportation, Telecom, Solar, Coatings, Irrigation and Ag Tech. We manufacture products in 85 facilities spread across six continents, and we do business in over 100 different countries. That’s what Valmont does.
We conserve resources and improve life, finding ways to make innovation and technology beneficial in the real world.
